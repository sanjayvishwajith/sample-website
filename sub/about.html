About Us

Started in 1968 with 25 rooms and other facilities,
The Serena Hotel stands today as an iconic landmark in Chennai
with 230 rooms, 8 food & beverage outlets, 10 conference & meeting venues and
a host of leisure facilities. It is a unit of Serena Industries Ltd,
which is more than four-decade strong multidisciplinary group of companies,
based in Chennai. Apart from having a strong hold in the Hospitality sector,
the management has diversified interests in mining, education, health and wellness as well



Serena Hotel has the privilege of being managed by the most experienced hoteliers and restaurateurs smt kavitha
Shri sanjana Shri. A.Devanand. The first family in Chennai hospitality business also has diversified interest
in restaurants, mining and education. Serena Hotel management is committed to promote tourism and
actively participates in the Government of India initiatives like HUNAR SE ROZGAR for skill development in hotel services.
Extending the reach through CSR activities, the management provides a platform for marginalised sections of society and the less privileged.
Serena Hotel is closely associated with National Association for Blind and organizes
several programs throughout the year like Blind Car Rally and fund raising campaigns.





Serena Hotel management firmly believes in ATITHI DEVO BHAVAH and always searches for excellence in everything
we do ... and the journey continues. Stay with us and enjoy a long tradition of impeccable service!
